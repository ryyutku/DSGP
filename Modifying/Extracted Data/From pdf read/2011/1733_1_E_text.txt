Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No.13 of 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No.13 of
1989, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this Order declare that:-
(a) The Excise duty on every article specified in Column III of the Schedule hereto shall be
payable at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of that Schedule.
(b) Wherever an ad valorem rate and a unit rate are specified in Column IV of Schedule hereto,
the applicable rate for the purpose of calculating the Excise duty payable shall be that rate the
application of which results in a higher amount being payable as Excise duty.
(d) The orders made under Section 3 and published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notifications No.
1702/19 of 21.04.2011, 1706/3 of 16.05.2011 and No. 1728/10 of 19.10.2011 are hereby
rescinded.
(e) This order shall come into effect from 22.11.2011
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance and Planning
