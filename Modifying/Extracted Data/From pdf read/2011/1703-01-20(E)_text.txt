Government Notifications
PORTS AND AIRPORTS DEVELOPMENT LEVY ACT No. 18 OF 2011
Order under Section 3
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Sub Section 3 of Section 3 of the Ports and Airports Development Levy Act No.18
of 2011, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning, do here by this Order declare that, the import of samples to
Sri Lanka in relation to business worth not more than Twenty Five Thousand Rupees subject to such terms and conditions
as prescribed by the Director General of Customs shall be exempted from the payment of Ports and Airports Development
Levy with effect from 26th April 2011.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
21st April 2011.
