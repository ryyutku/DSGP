PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virture of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I Mahinda Rajapaksa,
Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this Order declare that :
(a) The Excise duty on every article specified in Column III of the Schedule hereto shall be payable at the rate specified
in the corresponding entry in Column IV of that Schedule ;
(c) Wherever an advalorem rate and a unit rate are specified in Column IV of Schedule hereto, the applicable rate for the
purpose of calculating the Excise duty payable shall be that rate the application of which results in a higher amount
being payable as Excise duty ;
(d) The order made under Section 3 and pubished in the Extraordinary Gazette Notifications No. 1681/5 of 22.11.2010
and 1686/43 of 31.12.2010 is hereby rescinded ;
(e) This order shall come into effect from 24.04.2011.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
21st April, 2011.
