SRI LANKA EXPORT DEVELOPMENT ACT, No. 40 OF 1979
Order under Section 14
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 14 (1) of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Rishad
Bathiudeen, Minister of Industry and Commerce with the concurrence of the Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this
order decalre that,
(I) A Cess shall be charged, levied and paid on export of any article specified in the Column II and Column III, at the
rates specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV in the Schedule I hereto, with effect from 01st March , 2013.
The determitation of the Colombo Tea Auction average price mentioned in Column IV of that Schedule should be
as follows :
Colombo Tea Auction average price will be determined for each month by the Director General of Customs in
consultation with the Director General of Sri Lanka Tea Board and will come into effect from the first day of the
month. Colombo Tea Auction average price will be computed by taking the average prices of the Colombo Tea
Auction during the immediately preceding consecutive two months prior to one month from the effective date.
Accordingly, the repective Cess rates mentioned in the corresponding entry in Column IV, for articles specified
under HS Heading 09.02 mentioned in Column I of the Schedule of Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1783/20 dated
08.11.2012 are revised with effect from 01st March 2013.
(II) The Director General of Customs shall ensure that the relevant export proceeds are remitted through the banking
system.
and
(II) Cess rates mentioned, for every article specified under the HS Heading 41.07 in the Schedule II hereto, mentioned
in the Schedule of the Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1783/20 dated 08.11.2012, are hereby removed with effect from
23rd January, 2013.
RISHAD BATHIUDEEN,
Minister of Industry and Commerce.
Ministry of Industry and Commerce,
Colombo,
22nd January, 2013.
