EXCISE (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, NO. 13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act,
No. 13 of 1989, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this
Order declare that with effect from 01.09.2009, Excise Duty on every article specified in
Column III of the Schedule hereto shall be payable at the specified in the corresponding
entry in Column IV of that Schedule.
Wherever more than one rate is specified in Column IV of the Schedule hereto, the
applicable rate for the purpose of calculating the Excise Duty payable shall be that rate
application of which results in a higher amount being payable as Excise Duty.
Order made under Gazette Notification, No. 160817 of 30th June, 2009 is hereby
rescinded.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01
31st August, 2009.
