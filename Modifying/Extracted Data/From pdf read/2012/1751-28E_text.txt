Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No.13 of 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No.13 of 1989, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa,
Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this Order declare that:-
The Excise duty on every article specified in the Column II and III of the schedule hereto, correspoding to the every
article specified under the following H S Headings mentioned in the Column I of the schedule of the Extraordinay Gazette
Notification No. 1733/1 dated 21.11.2011 issued under the section 3 of this Act, shall be payable at the rate specified in the
corresponding entry in Column IV of the Schedule hereto, with effect from 31.03.2012.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSHA,
Minister of Finance and Planning
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
