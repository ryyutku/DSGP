Order under Section 3

No. 13 of 1989, I,Mahinda Rajapaksa , Minister of Finance and Planning, do hereby
rescind the order made under section 3 and published in Gazette Extraordinary , No.
1634/22 of 30th December, 2009 by the deletion, from the schedule to those orders of
the following articles.
