Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No.13 of 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No.13 of
1989, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this Order declare that:-
(a) The Excise duty on every article specified in Column III of the Schedule hereto shall be
payable at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of that Schedule,
(b) The Excise duty on import of vehicle in Column I and II of the Schedule under the
following H.S. Headings and H.S. Code by Government officers and the officials of the
statutory boards on concessionary duty rates in terms of the Trade, Tariff and Investment
Policy Circular No. 01 /2010 issued by the Secretary to the Treasury shall be payable at
the rate specified in Column V and Column VI respectively of that Schedule,
H.S. Headings 87.03
8703.21.69, 8703.21.92, 8703.22.59, 8703.22.79, 8703.23.52, 8703.23.59, 8703.23.92,
8703.31.70, 8703.31.91, 8703.32.51, 8703.32.59, 8703.32.71, 8703.32.79, 8703.32,91,
8703.32.94,
H.S. Headings 87.04
8704.21.91
shall be payable.
(c) Wherever an ad valorem rate and a unit rate are specified in Column IV of Schedule hereto,
the applicable rate for the purpose of calculating the Excise duty payable shall be that rate
the application of which results in a higher amount being payable as Excise duty.
(d) The order made under Section 3 and published in the Gazette Notifications No. 1574/8 of
06.11.2008, 1656/3 of 31.05.2010 and No. 1677/25 of 28.10.2010 are hereby rescinded.
(e) This order shall come into effect from 23.11.2010
