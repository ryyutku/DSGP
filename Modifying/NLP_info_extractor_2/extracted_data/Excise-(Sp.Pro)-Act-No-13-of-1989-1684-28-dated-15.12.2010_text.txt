EXCISE (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, NO. 13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13
of 1989, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning, do hereby rescind the order
made under section 3 and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1677/34 of 29th October, 2010
by deletion, from the schedule to those orders of the following articles with effect from
16.12.2010.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01
16th December, 2010.
